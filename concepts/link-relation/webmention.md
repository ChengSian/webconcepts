**[W3C TR http://www.w3.org/TR/webmention: Webmention](/specs/W3C/TR/webmention "Webmention is a simple way to notify any URL when you link to it on your site. From the receiver's perspective, it's a way to request notifications when other sites link to it."):** [Identifies a target URI that supports the Webmention protocol. This allows clients that mention a resource in some form of publishing process to contact that endpoint and inform it that this resource has been mentioned.](https://www.w3.org/TR/webmention/#sender-discovers-receiver-webmention-endpoint)
