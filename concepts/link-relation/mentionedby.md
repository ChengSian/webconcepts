**[Internet Draft snell-more-link-relations: Additional Link Relations and the urn:social Namespace](/specs/IETF/I-D/snell-more-link-relations "This specification defines a number of additional Link Relation Types that can used for a variety of purposes."):** [Refers to a resource that mentions the context resource in some fashion. This, for example, would be used when an article mentions another article, or a social status update mentions a particular user, etc.](http://tools.ietf.org/html/draft-snell-more-link-relations#section-3)
