**[RFC 6596: The Canonical Link Relation](/specs/IETF/RFC/6596 "RFC 5988 specifies a way to define relationships between links on the web. This document describes a new type of such a relationship, &#34;canonical&#34;, to designate an Internationalized Resource Identifier (IRI) as preferred over resources with duplicative content."):** [The target (canonical) IRI MUST identify content that is either duplicative or a superset of the content at the context (referring) IRI.](http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc6596#section-3)
